About PoultryDetect

Empowering farmers with AI-driven disease detection for better poultry health.

üéØ Project Overview

PoultryDetect is a transfer learning-based web and mobile platform designed to identify poultry diseases like Coccidiosis, New Castle Disease, and Salmonella through image inputs. The goal is to provide rapid diagnosis and reduce bird mortality using deep learning techniques such as VGG16.

üîç How It Works

  • AI-analyzes poultry images with a VGG16-based deep learning model.
  • Detects: Coccidiosis, New Castle Disease, Salmonella, or Healthy condition.
  • Returns quick diagnosis and treatment suggestions.

üåü Key Features

  • Fast, accurate AI predictions using Transfer Learning
  • Simple interface for farmers & veterinarians
  • Mobile & Web-based accessibility
  • Scenario-based diagnosis & suggestions
  • Supports JPG, PNG, JPEG formats

This system bridges the gap between advanced AI technology and hands-on poultry farming. It enables real-time disease management, helping reduce mortality, increase efficiency, and enhance training for veterinary students.

üì± Real-world Scenarios

Scenario 1: Outbreak in a Rural Community

Farmers diagnose Coccidiosis via the app after observing diarrhea and lethargy. Prompt treatment stops the disease from spreading.

Scenario 2: Commercial Poultry Farm Management

Daily checks detect New Castle Disease early, helping farm managers isolate infected birds and avoid costly losses.

Scenario 3: Veterinary Education and Research

Vet students use the app in training to simulate disease diagnosis, understand treatments, and practice real-time decision-making.
